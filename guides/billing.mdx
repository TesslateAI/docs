## Overview

Tesslate Studio offers flexible pricing with subscription plans and usage-based billing powered by Stripe.

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Current Plan

1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select **Settings** → **Billing**
3. View your current plan and usage

### Upgrading to Premium

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Select the Premium plan
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    Complete payment via Stripe secure checkout
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Your plan upgrades immediately. Premium features are now available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Canceling Subscription

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Select **Cancel Plan**
4. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period

<Warning>
  After cancellation, you'll lose access to premium features when your current billing period ends. Projects over the free tier limit will become read-only.
</Warning>

## Credits System

Credits are used for AI agent usage. Each AI model has different credit costs based on token usage.

### Viewing Credits

Your credit balance is shown in:
- The billing dashboard
- The chat interface (remaining credits indicator)

### Purchasing Credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Package">
    Choose a credit package:
    - **Small**: $5
    - **Medium**: $10
    - **Large**: $50
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Purchase">
    Pay via Stripe. Credits are added immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Usage Tracking

### Viewing Usage

The billing dashboard shows:
- AI token usage by model
- Project count vs. limit
- Deployment count vs. limit
- Historical usage charts

### Cost Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Monitor Usage">
    Check the billing dashboard regularly to track AI costs and avoid unexpected charges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Set Limits">
    Configure per-run cost limits in your agent settings to prevent runaway AI costs:
    - `AGENT_MAX_COST`: Maximum cost per agent run
    - `AGENT_MAX_ITERATIONS`: Maximum tool calls per run
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="robot" title="Choose Efficient Models">
    Smaller models (like GPT-4o-mini or Claude Haiku) are more cost-effective for simple tasks. Save powerful models for complex work.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payment Methods

Tesslate Studio accepts:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- Regional payment methods via Stripe

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click **Payment Methods**
3. Add, remove, or set default payment method

## Invoices & Receipts

### Viewing Invoices

All invoices are available in Settings → Billing → Transaction History.

### Download Receipt

Click any transaction to download a PDF receipt for expense reporting.

## Marketplace Purchases

### Buying from Marketplace

Purchase premium agents and bases from the Marketplace:

1. Browse the Marketplace
2. Click **Purchase** on any paid item
3. Complete Stripe checkout
4. Item is added to your Library immediately

### Creator Payouts

If you sell agents or bases on the Marketplace:
- 90% revenue share goes to creators
- 10% platform fee
- Payouts via Stripe Connect

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="Payment Failed">
    **Common causes:**
    - Insufficient funds
    - Card expired
    - Bank declined transaction

    **Solution:** Update your payment method in Settings → Billing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-exclamation" title="Credits Not Added">
    Credits should be added immediately after payment. If not:
    1. Refresh the page
    2. Check Settings → Billing → Transactions
    3. Contact support if the transaction shows as failed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="ban" title="Subscription Not Active">
    If your subscription isn't showing as active:
    1. Check for failed payment notifications
    2. Verify payment method is valid
    3. Contact support with your transaction ID
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
